# Field Signals For DL, Robotics, And Computer Vision

Use this as a starting point, not a frozen authority. If the user asks for reputation judgments, verify current facts on the internet.

## External Sources To Check

- Semantic Scholar: paper citation count, influential citation count, author h-index, author publications.
- Google Scholar Metrics: venue h5-index and h5-median for broad venue strength.
- CSRankings: institution strength from faculty publications at selective conferences.
- DBLP or official conference pages: venue/year verification.
- Author, lab, project, or institution pages: affiliation and project status.
- GitHub/project pages: code/model/data availability and adoption.

Useful public pages:

- `https://csrankings.org/` - metrics-based CS institution ranking from selective conferences.
- `https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=top_venues&hl=en&vq=eng_artificialintelligence`
- `https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=top_venues&hl=en&vq=eng_computervisionpatternrecognition`
- `https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=top_venues&hl=en&vq=eng_robotics`
- `https://api.semanticscholar.org/api-docs/graph`

## Venue Priors

Top-tier default priors:

- Deep learning / ML: NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR.
- Computer vision: CVPR, ICCV, ECCV.
- Robotics learning: RSS, CoRL.

Strong but inspect-paper-carefully priors:

- Robotics: ICRA, IROS, RA-L, T-RO, IJRR, Science Robotics.
- AI/ML adjacent: AAAI, IJCAI, AISTATS, KDD, ACL/EMNLP/NAACL when language is central.
- Vision journals: TPAMI, IJCV.

Treat workshops, arXiv-only papers, and technical reports as provisional unless other signals are excellent.

## Institution And Lab Priors

Reputation should be field-specific and verified. A seed list that often deserves a closer look in DL/CV/robotics includes:

- universities: Stanford, UC Berkeley, CMU, MIT, University of Washington, UIUC, Georgia Tech, UT Austin, Princeton, Cornell, ETH Zurich, EPFL, Oxford, Cambridge, Toronto, Montreal/Mila, Tsinghua, Peking, HKUST, Zhejiang, Shanghai Jiao Tong
- industry/research labs: Google DeepMind, Google Research, OpenAI, Meta AI/FAIR, Microsoft Research, NVIDIA, Toyota Research Institute, Physical Intelligence, Boston Dynamics AI Institute, Adobe Research, Apple ML Research

This list is only a recall aid. Verify the specific paper's authors and affiliations. A famous institution outside the exact topic should receive only a small boost.

## Author Reputation Checks

Prefer current, paper-specific evidence:

- Semantic Scholar or Google Scholar profile shows high-impact prior work in the same area.
- Author has repeated papers at the relevant top venues.
- Author/lab created widely used datasets, benchmarks, robot platforms, codebases, or models.
- Author won field-relevant awards or best paper awards.
- The current paper is already cited by or compared against by strong follow-up work.

Avoid vague claims like "famous lab" without a source. If uncertain, write "author signal unclear" rather than guessing.
